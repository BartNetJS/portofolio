ATPC / VTTI Terminal Automation

DeltaV Control System Integration Production

Engagement Overview

Production-grade DeltaV integration platform operating at the ATPC / VTTI petroleum terminal. The system bridges Emerson DeltaV control hardware with terminal back-office data, orchestrating safe, real-time truck loading, badge authentication, recipe synchronization, and tank inventory visibility.

  • Terminal Automation - End-to-end workflow from badge scan to valve control across 8 loading bays
  • DeltaV Gateway - OPC DA bridge connecting field devices with SQL Server back-end systems
  • Order and Recipe Sync - Automated synchronization of products, tanks, blends, and loading limits
  • Operational Safety - Embedded interlocks, watchdog recovery, and audit trails for every transaction
  • Offline Resilience - Graceful degradation with cached authorizations when central systems are unavailable
  • 15+ Years in Production - Continuous 24/7 operation within the VTTI terminal environment

Terminal Operations Portfolio

Loading and Access Control

Integrated workflows that authorize drivers, validate orders, and execute secure loading sequences inside DeltaV.

Truck Loading Workflow

Event-driven orchestration of CSN scan, badge resolution, order lookup, setpoint calculation, and pump/valve control.

Badge and Access Management

CSN-to-ID conversion, customer-level authorization, and offline badge issuance to keep loading bays available.

Tank and Recipe Synchronization

Automatic sync of tank assignments, blend percentages, and product recipes from back-office databases into DeltaV.
