About Codeware

Codeware is an enterprise AI development company headquartered in Belgium and serving clients across Europe. We bridge the gap between proof-of-concept and production by building autonomous AI agents, robust RAG systems, and Azure-based cloud platforms.

With 40+ years of engineering experience and deep expertise in the Microsoft stack, our team designs secure, compliant AI solutions that meet GDPR and EU AI Act expectations. We partner long-term with enterprises to modernize systems, orchestrate LLM workflows, and deliver measurable outcomes.

AI Agent Development Services

Codeware specializes in building autonomous AI agents that go beyond proof-of-concept to deliver production-ready enterprise solutions. Using cutting-edge frameworks like Semantic Kernel and AutoGen, we develop intelligent agents capable of complex reasoning, task execution, and seamless integration with your existing systems.

Our AI Agent Capabilities

  • Autonomous Workflow Agents - AI systems that analyze data and execute tasks independently
  • RAG-Powered Assistants - Zero-hallucination chatbots trained on your internal documentation
  • LLM Orchestration - Multi-model pipelines with evaluation and monitoring
  • Enterprise Integration - Secure deployment within your existing infrastructure

Based in Belgium, we serve enterprise clients across Europe who need reliable, scalable AI solutions that meet strict security and compliance requirements. Explore our AI case studies to see how we deliver measurable business outcomes.

Production-Ready RAG Systems & LLM Orchestration

We design Retrieval Augmented Generation platforms with robust evaluation pipelines, grounding, and monitoring to eliminate hallucinations. Our engineers combine Azure OpenAI, vector databases, and observability tooling to build transparent knowledge assistants that keep stakeholders in control.

Engagements include data ingestion pipelines, secure document enrichment, red-teaming, and governance processes so you can deploy conversational AI that respects enterprise standards.
